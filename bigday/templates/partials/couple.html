The Couple

From Shreya

In Sid, I first found my best friend, my human diary and then the love of my life and my other half. He is my pillow, pillar and punching bag, all rolled into one. We have known each other for over eight years now, dated for five, and been engaged for two. From being a bunch of teenagers sharing mindless jokes and laughter and secrets to being two people deeply in love sharing dreams and hopes and thoughts and plans about their life together, I have hopped on the most beautiful adventure of my life with him. I cannot wait to commit to a lifetime of laughter, surprises, adventures, bad dance moves and off-key singing, disagreements, holding on and never letting go with my best friend and the most annoying, loving, goofy, perfect person I know.

From Siddharth

The first time I met Shreya in 2009, I felt a connection and I knew that she is going to be the one. And I feel so fortunate that everything worked out perfectly. She is like this ideal beauty with brains, who almost always knows the right thing to do and is always trying to raise the bar for the right thing too. Over the last 8 years of togetherness, we have gone through a rollercoaster ride and seen each other at our best and worst and every moment, my love for her has only grown. From being my best friend to girlfriend, classmate to colleague, girlfriend to fiancée, I have cherished every moment spent with her! Now, I can’t wait for the transition from fiancée to wifey and hop onto the beautiful adventure that awaits us!
