The Couple

Shreya Ghelani

In Sid, I first found my best friend, my human diary and then the love of my life and my other half. He is my pillow, pillar and punching bag, all rolled into one. We have known each other for over eight years now, dated for five, and been engaged for two. From being a bunch of teenagers sharing mindless jokes and laughter and secrets to being two people deeply in love sharing dreams and hopes and thoughts and plans about their life together, I have hopped on the most beautiful adventure of my life with him. I cannot wait to commit to a lifetime of laughter, surprises, adventures, bad dance moves and off-key singing, disagreements, holding on and never letting go with my best friend and the most annoying, loving, goofy, perfect person I know.

Siddharth Shah

Spark MicroGrants is a small NGO pioneering a new method of community development. Spark supports rural poor communities to design, implement and manage their own social impact projects. They provide micro-grants of between $2,000-$10,000 to enable project implementation, such as of a school, electricity line or farm. No interest or repayment of the funds granted is requested. To date Spark has partnered with over 100 communities in Rwanda, Uganda, and Burundi who have developed local project plans and are in the process of implementing them.
