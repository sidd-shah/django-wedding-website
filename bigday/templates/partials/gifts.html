Gifts

The greatest gift we can receive are your continued friendship and your presence at the wedding. We have been fortunate to benefit in love and life. However, not everyone is. So, we want to take this opportunity to make a difference, however small that might be, in the lives of the lesser privileged.

Shreya's father has been associated with a charity group(Shree Vardhman Yuvak Mandal,Borivali) that provides at-home medical equipment at zero rent for patients/families who cannot afford these. This aid relieves them of the financial stress at a time when they are already battling extremely painful medical situations.

We believe that education is the most effective weapon against poverty and the surest door to opportunity. We have always been promoters of education and on this special day, we would like to make a donation to an NGO that we believe does phenomenal work in imparting education - Make A Difference (MAD)

So, we ask that if you are considering a gift, we would be honored by a donation towards these causes in lieu of a traditional gift. We thank you for your generosity.
